About Us

There is a common purpose behind our endeavours, to create an elegant, yet welcoming ambience where all of your needs are anticipated and all of your expectations are surpassed. We care for and cherish local traditions and we take pride in combining local characteristics with attentive service, in tasteful and luxurious settings. Sourabh Choudhary built the Nettech International Hotel in the 2000 for his friends but the hotel has been frequented by celebrities ever since. It was one of the first hotels in the City of Joy. Our aim is to make your travel experience a genuine pleasure and to share with you our unique brand of ‘local' hospitality.

The Nettech International Hotel . . .

Offers a unique collection of luxury hotel that boast a warm welcome and opulent ambiance throughout the world. Known for our quality and unique sense of style, Nettech International Hotel offer guests, who are looking for out of this world travel experiences, the perfect balance of quality, authenticity, luxury standards and prime location. We are proud of our passion, our people and our commitment to delivering the ultimate hotel experience.
